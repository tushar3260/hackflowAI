# HackFlow AI — Client User Guide

## 1. What is HackFlow AI?
HackFlow AI is an advanced platform designed to make running hackathons effortless and exciting. It helps you organize events, manage teams, accept project submissions, and evaluate them fairly using both human judges and AI-powered insights.

Whether you are hosting a small internal coding challenge or a large-scale global hackathon, HackFlow AI handles everything from registration to the final leaderboard.

## 2. User Types
There are three main roles in HackFlow AI, each with specific permissions:

| Role | best For | What They Can Do |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Organizer** | Event Managers, Admins | Create hackathons, invite judges, manage teams, view all submissions, control demo mode. |
| **Participant** | Hackers, Developers | Join hackathons, create/join teams, submit projects, view their own scores and AI feedback. |
| **Judge** | Industry Experts, Mentors | View assigned submissions, grade projects, see AI analysis, submit evaluations. |

## 3. Access Links & Navigation
Here are the main pages you will use:

*   **/** (Landing Page): The public home page introducing the platform.
*   **/login**: Sign in to your account.
*   **/register**: Create a new account.
*   **/dashboard**: Your personal control center. This changes based on your role (Organizer, Participant, or Judge).
*   **/profile**: Manage your personal details and avatar.
*   **/leaderboard/[id]**: The live scoreboard showing team rankings.

## 4. Common First Steps

### Initial Setup
1.  **Register:** Go to `/register`. Enter your name, email, and choose a password.
2.  **Select Role:** During registration, select whether you are an **Organizer** or a **Participant**. *(Note: Judges are usually invited by Organizers, but can also register directly if allowed)*.
3.  **Login:** Use your email and password at `/login`.
4.  **Dashboard:** You will be automatically redirected to your Dashboard.

## 5. Organizer Flow
*How to run a hackathon*

1.  **Create Hackathon:**
    *   On your Dashboard, click **"Create Hackathon"**.
    *   Fill in the event name, dates, and description.
    *   Click **"Create"**.

2.  **Setup Rounds & Scoring:**
    *   Go to the **"Manage Hackathon"** page.
    *   Add rounds (e.g., "Qualifier", "Finals").
    *   Define judging criteria (e.g., "Innovation", "Design", "Code Quality").

3.  **Invite Judges:**
    *   In the **"Judges"** section, enter the email addresses of the people you want to judge your event.
    *   They will receive an invitation or be linked if they already have an account.

4.  **Manage Teams:**
    *   View the **"Teams"** tab to see who has registered.
    *   You can approve or remove teams if necessary.

5.  **Monitor Submissions:**
    *   Go to **"Submissions"** to see all uploaded projects.
    *   You can filter by status (Submitted, Evaluated, Pending).

6.  **View Leaderboard:**
    *   Click **"Leaderboard"** to see real-time rankings.
    *   You can toggle **"Transparency Mode"** to show or hide detailed breakdowns to participants.

## 6. Participant Flow
*How to compete*

1.  **Join or Create Team:**
    *   On your Dashboard, you will see active hackathons.
    *   Click **"Join"** on an event.
    *   You can then **"Create a Team"** (you become the captain) or **"Join a Team"** using a unique team code provided by a friend.

2.  **Submit Project:**
    *   When the submission period is open, go to your **"Team Panel"**.
    *   Click **"Submit Project"**.
    *   Enter your project title, description, and upload files or links (GitHub, Demo URL).
    *   Click **"Submit"**.

3.  **View AI Feedback:**
    *   Immediately after submission, our AI analyzes your project.
    *   Check your dashboard to see **"AI Insights"**—helpful tips on how to improve your submission before judging starts.

4.  **Check Results:**
    *   Visit the **Leaderboard** to see where your team stands.
    *   Click on your team name to see your score breakdown (Judges vs. AI).

## 7. Judge Flow
*How to evaluate*

1.  **Access Assignments:**
    *   Log in and go to your **Judge Dashboard**.
    *   You will see a list of hackathons you are assigned to.
    *   Click **"Start Judging"**.

2.  **Evaluate Submissions:**
    *   You will see a list of projects pending review.
    *   Click **"Evaluate"** on a project.

3.  **Review & Score:**
    *   Read the description and view the project links.
    *   **AI Access:** Look at the **"AI Analysis"** panel for an objective quality summary to help you decide.
    *   Use the sliders/inputs to score based on the criteria (0-10 or 0-100).
    *   Add optional written feedback.

4.  **Submit Score:**
    *   Click **"Submit Evaluation"**. The leaderboard updates instantly.

## 8. Profile Management
*   Click your **Avatar** in the top right corner.
*   Select **"Profile"**.
*   **Edit Details:** Update your display name or email.
*   **Avatar:** Click the image to upload a new profile picture.
*   **Security:** Update your password here.

## 9. Dashboard Explanation
Your dashboard gives you a quick overview:

*   **Active Events:** Hackathons currently running.
*   **Stats Cards:**
    *   *Participants:* Total registered users.
    *   *Teams:* Active teams.
    *   *Submissions:* Projects turned in.
    *   *Pending Reviews:* Submissions waiting for a judge.
*   **Recent Activity:** A log of what just happened (e.g., "Team Alpha submitted a project").

## 10. Leaderboard Page
This is the heart of the competition:

*   **Rank:** Current standing (1st, 2nd, 3rd...).
*   **Team Name:** Who is leading.
*   **Total Score:** The final weighted score (combination of Judge scores + AI scores).
*   **Rounds:** Shows progress through different stages.
*   **Breakdown:** Click the arrow (`▼`) to see exactly how points were awarded.

## 11. Demo Mode (If Enabled)
If your platform is in **Demo Mode**, you can simulate a hackathon instantly:

1.  **Open Demo Panel:** Found on the Organizer Dashboard.
2.  **Generate Data:** Click **"Seed Data"** to create fake users, teams, and submissions.
3.  **Simulate Activity:** Click **"Simulate Round"** to have the AI automatically grade projects and generate rankings.
4.  **Walkthrough:** Click the **"Help"** or **"Tour"** icon to get a guided tour of the features.

## 12. Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Cannot Login** | Check your email/password. Ensure you registered with the correct email. |
| **Submission Locked** | The submission deadline may have passed. Contact the organizer. |
| **Cannot Join Team** | Verify the **Team Code**. Teams may also be full (max members reached). |
| **No Scores Visible** | The organizer may have hidden the leaderboard, or judging hasn't started yet. |
