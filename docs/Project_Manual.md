## üõ† For Organizers

As an Organizer, you have full control over the lifecycle of events you create.

### 1. Organizer Dashboard
Your central hub shows real-time analytics:
*   **Total Participants**: How many users have joined.
*   **Teams Formed**: Number of groups competing.
*   **Submissions**: Projects currently uploaded.

### 2. Creating a Hackathon
*   Click **"Create Hackathon"**.
*   Fill in details like **Event Title**, **Theme**, **Description**, and **Dates**.
*   Set the **Difficulty Level** (Beginner, Intermediate, Pro).

### 3. Managing the Event
*   **Access Control**: You can only view and edit hackathons that *you* have created.
*   **Judges**: Invite experts to help score submissions.
*   **Teams**: View a list of all teams and their members.
*   **Submissions**: Monitor incoming projects. You can view files (Presentations, Documents) uploaded by teams.

### 4. Leaderboard
*   Once judging is complete, you can generate and **Publish the Leaderboard**.
*   This announces the winners to all participants.

## üíª For Participants

Join the competition and showcase your skills.

### 1. Finding Events
*   Visit the **Landing Page** to browse "Active Hackathons".
*   Click on a card to see details like deadline, rounds, and theme.

### 2. Joining & Teams
*   **Register**: Click "Join Hackathon" on the event page.
*   **Form a Team**:
    *   **Create**: Start a new team and get a unique **Team Code**.
    *   **Join**: Enter a code from a friend to join their team.

### 3. Submitting Your Project
*   Go to the **Submission Panel**.
*   Upload your work files (e.g., PowerPoint presentations, PDF reports).
*   **Important**: Ensure you submit before the deadline!
